    How We Work

    From idea to installation

    A clear design and build journey for homeowners - from first consultation, measurement, design proposal, production, to professional installation.

    1. I
      First conversation

      Consultation

      We discuss your space, needs, lifestyle, style direction, timeline, and budget.

    2. II
      Technical check

      Survey & Measurement

      Our team checks the space, takes precise measurements, and studies technical requirements.

    3. III
      Design direction

      Design Proposal

      We prepare layout direction, material options, moodboard, and quotation for your approval.

    4. IV
      Custom build

      Production

      Your custom furniture is produced with careful detailing, material control, and refined finishing.

    5. V
      Final details

      Installation & Handover

      Our team installs the interior on site, checks every detail, and hands over the completed space.
