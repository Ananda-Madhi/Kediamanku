Our story

Turning houses into personal living spaces.

Every home has its own rhythm. Kediamanku begins by understanding how homeowners live, store, move, rest, cook, and gather - then translates those needs into custom interiors with calm proportions, refined materials, and precise production.

Our mission is simple: create interiors that are beautiful enough to feel special, practical enough for daily life, and durable enough to be trusted for years.

Why we're different

Design expertise with build responsibility.

Kediamanku combines concept, technical detailing, production direction, and installation control in one integrated workflow so the final result stays aligned with the design.

Measured for real life

Layouts are planned around room size, habits, storage needs, and long-term use.

Material-led decisions

Colors, textures, hardware, and finishing are chosen for both beauty and durability.

Production-aware design

Ideas are developed with technical build feasibility, precision, and installation flow in mind.
